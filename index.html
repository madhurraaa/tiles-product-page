Medium SELECT is a special and particularly high-quality selection of the popular medium travertine. Characteristic of this extremely popular travertine is its special, Mediterranean-looking color harmony with few fluctuations and veins. In addition, Medium SELECT is particularly strong for a travertine and has significantly fewer and smaller holes, if you compare this with other travertines on the market.

Medium SELECT: pure relaxation

The high-quality material selection emphasizes the Mediterranean effect of the stone - in contrast to our somewhat more rustic travertine medium - again clearly.

The tiles have a light cream-colored tone, the laid surface looks almost marble-like and overall very harmonious. A feel-good stone for cozy seating areas! A comparable travertine was installed in the Colosseum in Rome. So expect a very long shelf life for your beautiful garden terrace.

More rustic, natural alternative

If you like it a little more rustic and natural, simply choose the more intensely colored - and certainly no less beautiful - sort of our regular travertine medium . Also a good alternative: our porcelain stoneware terrace slabs in stone look Siena and Verona !

This rustic sorting is also known by other suppliers under the trade names Classic Beige and Classico, sometimes also under Travertine Beige or, due to its processing, under Travertine Antique.
